## About

I am **Tran Hoang Hai**, a **Software Engineering D22 student at Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology (PTIT)**, currently shaping my profile toward **backend engineering**.

I focus on building software that is clear in structure, practical in business logic, and readable for both developers and reviewers. My current direction is centered on **Java**, **Spring Boot**, **REST APIs**, **database workflows**, and authentication.

> **Clarity over exaggeration. Structure over noise. Progress over performance.**
